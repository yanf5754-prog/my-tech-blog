Melamine Paper Quality Issues: Practical Case Series 1

Root Cause Analysis

  • Worm-like Scorch Marks: Wood pulp fiber mismatch + slow press closing speed causing phase change before pressing.
  • Bubbles/Sticking: Reduced ink binder (to resolve silver powder conflict) led to poor resin penetration.
  • Silver Powder Drift: Insufficient binder failed to fix silver powder effectively under resin impact.

Solutions

  • Reduce volatile content.
  • Alter resin curing logic (not just time).
  • Enhance resin penetration depth into base paper and ink layers.

Bản tiếng Việt

Giải pháp: Giảm chất bay hơi; Thay đổi logic đóng rắn của nhựa; Tăng cường thẩm thấu vào lớp giấy đế và mực.
